A Lavanya Honoured With Teaching Excellence Awards 2018

An award is something given to a person in recognition of his excellence in a certain field. Teaching Excellence Awards 2018 (TEA - 2018) was an effort of MT Research and Educational Services (MTRES) in association with Buzzingg and Indian Achievers Story to recognition the best teachers (Nation Builders) for their special contribution in respective domain. Meet one of the awardees, A. Lavanya, who has been honoured with TEA-2018 as Promising Teacher.



Dr. G. Ravi was honoured with MTRES Teaching Excellence Award 2018

Teachers are the soul base of the development of a country and today we proudly bring to you the story of a lady who is a mentor to her students, an inspiration to millions and above all a noble human being. A. Lavanya a resident of Chennai is currently working as an Assistant Professor at SRM University, Chennai. She holds specialization in Electrical and Electronics Engineering. She is a teacher to many but is still continuing to gain knowledge. She is pursuing Ph.D in Power Electronics from SRM University. Her strength of knowledge cannot be questioned, she has pursued B.E. from University of Madras, M.E. from Sathyabama University and is a TQM Certified teacher from SRM University. Before working as Assistant Professor for SRM University, she used to work as a Lecturer for Sri Balaji Polytechnic College from 2001 – 2002 and for Tagore Engineering College from 2002 – 2007. She has attended 5 conference and 24 workshops, STTP and FDP combined. She has done commendable work in the field of research inclusive of subjects like DC-DC Converter, Renewable Energy, and Microgrid. She has published more than 20 papers in international journal and conference. She has organized community service programs targeting unemployed youth, women and school children. She is life member of ISTE, IE(I) and annual member of IEEE and ISCA. She is Coordinator of NAAC Department Level, Institutional Central Coordinator, UGC Department. Her vital and inevitable role in the society cannot be neglected and will forever be honoured.
