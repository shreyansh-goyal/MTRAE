Aashish A. Bardekar Honoured With Teaching Excellence Awards 2018

An award is something given to a person in recognition of his excellence in a certain field. Teaching Excellence Awards 2018 (TEA - 2018) was an effort of MT Research and Educational Services (MTRES)  in association with Buzzingg and Indian Achievers Story to recognition the best teachers (Nation Builders) for their special contribution in respective domain. Meet one of the awardees, Mr. Aashish A. Bardekar, who has been honoured with TEA-2018 as Promising Teacher.



MTRES is doing an excellent work by motivating and honouring researchers like me with an award thereby appreciating and giving recognition. I am thankful for this wonderful gesture.
Aashish Bardekar



Aashish A. Bardekar was honoured with MTRES Teaching Excellence Award 2018

Mr. Aashish A. Bardekar is working as an Assistant Professor for Sipna College of Engineering and Technology, Amravati. Currently pursuing Ph.D, he is an M.E. in Computer Science from Amravati University.

IOSRD Best Research Award 2018


He has been rewarded with many awards including Best Research Award, International Research Awards on Engineering, Science & Management, IOSRD, Vishakhapattanam, Andhra Pradesh

IJRULA Best Research Award 2018


He has been awarded Best Research Award by International Journal for Research under Literal Access (IJRULA), 19 June 2018, Trichy, Tamil Nadu. His name has been associated with the publication of 30 referred journals, 16 papers presented in conferences, 15 STTP and 8 seminars. He held other responsibilities like he was the Stock Verification In-charge, Youth Festival Manager, Centralised Parent Meet In-charge, University Theory Exam In-charge, University Practical Exam In-charge, University College Samanvayak. He also owns membership for various scientific and professional societies, and is also a vital member of the Lion Club International and Spicmacay. It is absolutely vital to mention that he has also been elected for Outstanding Scientist Award 2018 and Best National Research Scholar Award 2018. With an amazing and inspiring journey of 14 years we look forward to the year coming ahead of this time.
